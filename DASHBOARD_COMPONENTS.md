## Key Design Decisions

### Why Neumorphism + Glassmorphism?
- Modern aesthetic
- Professional appearance
- Soft on the eyes (long viewing sessions)
- Depth without harsh shadows
- Follows current design trends

### Why Soft Blue/Purple Palette?
- Professional and trustworthy
- Tech industry standard
- Good contrast for accessibility
- Easy on eyes
- Calming and focused

### Why Large Border Radius (24-30px)?
- Modern, friendly appearance
- Reduces visual hierarchy dominance
- Creates softer, less aggressive UI
- Matches iOS/macOS design language

### Why Generous Spacing?
- Reduces cognitive load
- Improves readability
- Makes interactions feel premium
- Better for mobile usability
- Professional appearance
